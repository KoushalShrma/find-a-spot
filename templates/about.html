Our Story

Founded in 2023, Find-A-Spot began as a simple idea to solve the frustrating experience of finding parking in crowded cities. Our team of parking enthusiasts and tech experts came together to create a solution that would save people time and reduce urban congestion.

Today, we serve our customers, helping them find and reserve parking spots in seconds. Our mission is to eliminate the stress of parking so you can focus on what really matters.
